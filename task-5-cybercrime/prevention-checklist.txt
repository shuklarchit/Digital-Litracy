Prevention Checklist

• Never share your OTP or UPI PIN with anyone  
• Do not approve unknown payment requests  
• Always verify the sender before making payments  
• Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
• Use strong passwords and always activate two-factor authentication  
• Always check your bank statements  
• Never scan unknown QR codes  
• Only use trusted and secure payment applications  
• Report fraud cases at cybercrime.gov.in  
• Call the helpline number 1930 in case of fraud
