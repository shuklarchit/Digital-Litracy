# Digital-Literacy

Task 1


In making the Digital Literacy Awareness poster, I used Canva, which is an easy and convenient online tool for making designs. It is user-friendly and allows you to pick a good template and add icons and other information in a neat manner without having to be good at making designs.

In this activity, I made a one-page infographic for a poster titled “Digital Literacy Basics.” The poster aims to inform people of the meaning of digital literacy and the various digital tools that students need to know, such as cloud storage and coding sites. It also aims to inform people of the various safe internet practices to be followed, such as the use of passwords and protection of personal information.

In making the poster, I found it quite interesting to look for various design aspects and layouts to be used for the poster. On the other hand, I also experienced a bit of challenge in making sure that there is a good balance of visual and textual information without making the poster look too crowded.
